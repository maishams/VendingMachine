About Amazon Vending Machine at LHR16

Welcome to the Amazon Vending Machine portal for the LHR16 office!

Our innovative approach combines the convenience of traditional vending machines with the power of modern technology. Here's how it works:

  1. Decide What You Need: Browse through a wide range of IT equipment.
  2. Reserve Your Equipment: Reserve the items you need without waiting.
  3. Collect from the Vending Machine: Pick up your reserved items from the LHR16 office vending machine.

This service is exclusively for our LHR16 colleagues. We aim to make your work life easier by ensuring you have the tools you need, when you need them.

While the items from the vending machine may be free for employees, it's important to remember that they come at a cost to Amazon. We encourage all employees to be frugal and only take items when necessary.
